Component Analysis

Thrust (T)

--

Growth momentum and capital generation velocity

Derived from:
  • Revenue growth rate
  • Operating cash flow / Revenue
  • Reinvestment rate

Efficiency (E)

--

Asset productivity and operational excellence

Derived from:
  • Asset turnover ratio
  • Return on assets (ROA)
  • Operating margin

Slack (S)

--

Liquidity buffer and financial resilience

Derived from:
  • Current ratio
  • Cash to revenue ratio
  • Debt capacity headroom

Drag (D)

--

Friction forces impeding regeneration

Derived from:
  • Debt service ratio
  • Interest burden
  • Operating inefficiency
